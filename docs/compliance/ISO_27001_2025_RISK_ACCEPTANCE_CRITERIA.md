# ISO/IEC 27001 Risk Acceptance Criteria (P2)

Version: 0.1  
Last updated: 2026-03-04  
Status: Draft for management approval

## Purpose

Define the thresholds and governance rules for accepting, mitigating, transferring, or avoiding risks in the ISO/IEC 27001 readiness scope.

## Risk Acceptance Matrix

| Inherent risk level | Allowed action | Required condition |
|---|---|---|
| High | Mitigate or avoid | Open treatment action with dated owner and weekly review |
| Medium | Mitigate or accept with conditions | Compensating control and explicit sign-off record |
| Low | Accept | Owner acknowledgment and periodic review |

## Approval Authority

| Risk level | Primary approver | Secondary approver |
|---|---|---|
| High | Management representative | Security lead |
| Medium | Security lead | Product owner |
| Low | Control owner | Security lead (notification only) |

## Mandatory Acceptance Record Fields

1. Risk ID and statement.
2. Current severity and rationale.
3. Decision: mitigate, accept, transfer, avoid.
4. Expiry date for accepted risk.
5. Linked evidence and compensating controls.

## Review Cadence

- High risk: weekly review until closure.
- Medium risk: bi-weekly review.
- Low risk: monthly review.
